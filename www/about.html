ABOUT

Welcome to the Parramatta Park Trust’s Heritage Mobile App!

Through this app you can take a glimpse inside one of Australia’s oldest buildings and explore its rich heritage right from the palm of your hand!

This app was developed, in conjunction with Western Sydney University students, as a creative storytelling tool and aims to promote the cultural landscape of Parramatta Park and conserve the integrity of the Dairy Precinct for future generations.

The Dairy Precinct is the area of land found in the centre of World Heritage Listed Old Government House and Domain (Parramatta Park), and contains the Dairy Cottage and Ranger’s Cottage. The Precinct is bound by timber fencing, and is located to the north of Old Government House, overlooking the Parramatta River. Major upgrades of the Dairy Precinct were completed in 2016 including the creation of a central exhibition space, new digital media systems and landscape works.

Uncover the unique heritage on a self-guided virtual tour of the Dairy Precinct with historic and contemporary images and 360° interactive panoramas that allow for a unique way to experience in-app footage as if you were standing within the walls of the historic buildings from the comfort of your own home.

To book a real life tour of the Dairy Precinct or find out more about Parramatta Park and its history go to our website www.parrapark.com.au or visit our Facebook page.
