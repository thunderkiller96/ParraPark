Dairy Cottage Garden
Surrounding the Dairy and Rangers Cottages is the beautiful Dairy Cottage Garden featuring manicured lawns, sandstone paths, a majestic Fig tree and elegant Jacaranda trees overlooking the Parramatta River. A vegetable and herb garden remains tended to and an interpretive metal cow sculpture represents the Dairy cows that would have lived there.
